Here I am in my 20s trying to find my place in this world as an artist. This life hasn’t been an easy one, and to the surprise of nobody more then myself I’m still kicking in this struggle to thrive.

 

          My name is Maynard Mann, a first name that I keep only because it sounds pretentious enough, and a last name I keep only because I’ve signed way to many damn pieces of art with it already.

 

          I have experience in a wide variety of mediums from carpentry to adobe illustrator but at this stage in my life, I’ve fallen for my Prismacolor colored pencils and spend very little time coveting any other mistress without proper cause.

 

          Inspiration for my work is drawn from many different sources at all times. But key stones in my mental forge include but are not limited to-

 

 Horrible music from the barren cultural waste-land that is the early 2000s that I’m wholly confident nobody else listens to unironically such as M.S.I, Dog Fashion Disco, Nachtmahr to name a few.

 

 The unceasing ever-pervasive crushing weight of existence that I bear upon my shoulders every waking moment that I preside over this never ending hell scape of nightmares that we collectively refer to as life on Earth.

 

 The imaginings of H.P. Lovecraft or more accurately the derivative works that permeate pop culture this day in age and other works of dark fantasy.

 

And last but not least the color struck brain vomit this is the psychedelic art boom of the 70’s and onward.
